## Aesthetic Guidelines
- **Primary Motif**: The Box (The Vessel).
- **Style**: Pop Art / Comic Book Industrial.
- **Symbolism**: The Penguin (Pingu) at the center of the soul-core.
- **Dots**: Representing both the digital bits and Lichtenstein's signature style.
