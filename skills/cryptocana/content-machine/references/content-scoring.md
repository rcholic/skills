# Content Scoring Rubric

## How to Use
Before delivering ANY piece of content, score it against this rubric.
If total score < 70, revise before delivering.
Share the score with the user so they understand the quality bar.

## Quick Self-Review Checklist
Before scoring, run through these:
- [ ] First sentence would stop the scroll
- [ ] No paragraph longer than 4 lines
- [ ] Every sentence earns its place (cut the rest)
- [ ] Specific details, not vague claims ("3x more" not "much better")
- [ ] Ends with ONE clear action, not three
- [ ] Reads like a human wrote it, not a robot
- [ ] No filler phrases ("In conclusion", "It's important to note", "As we can see")
- [ ] Emotion present (curiosity, surprise, relief, excitement, or validation)

## Platform-Specific Bonus Criteria

### Twitter/X Thread
- Bonus +5: Each tweet stands alone (can be screenshot-shared)
- Bonus +5: Last tweet has a strong retweet hook

### LinkedIn
- Bonus +5: Personal story in opening
- Bonus +5: Invites comments with a question

### Blog Post
- Bonus +5: Subheadings tell the whole story on their own
- Bonus +5: Includes at least one surprising stat or insight

### Newsletter
- Bonus +5: Subject line creates genuine curiosity
- Bonus +5: Has a personal "from the editor" feel
