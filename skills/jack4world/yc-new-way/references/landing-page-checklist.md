# Landing Page Checklist (minimal, conversion-first)

## Above the fold
- [ ] One-sentence promise: **what + for who + outcome**
- [ ] One primary CTA (button)
- [ ] Social proof / credibility (even small: source links, logos, numbers)

## Trust
- [ ] Clear disclosure (affiliate/ads) if applicable
- [ ] Contact/about page or footer identity
- [ ] Evidence links (sources) for claims

## Offer clarity
- [ ] What’s included / what’s not
- [ ] Who it’s for / not for
- [ ] FAQ: 5 questions max

## Measurement
- [ ] Pageview + CTA click tracking
- [ ] Outbound click tracking
- [ ] Optional: email capture tracking

## Speed
- [ ] Loads fast on mobile
- [ ] No heavy JS unless needed
